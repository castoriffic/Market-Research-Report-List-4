<p><p>Prefabricated buildings are categorized into different types based on their structural systems. The Beam and Column System involves the use of vertical columns and horizontal beams for support. The Floor and Roof System includes pre-assembled floor and roof elements. The Bearing Wall System relies on load-bearing walls for support. The Facade System focuses on the exterior walls and facade elements. Each system offers unique advantages in terms of construction speed, cost-effectiveness, and design flexibility in the prefabricated buildings market.</p></p>
