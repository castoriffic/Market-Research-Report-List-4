<p><ul><li>Surface Resistivity Meter</li><li>Volume Resistivity Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital resistivity meters come in two main types - surface resistivity meter and volume resistivity meter. Surface resistivity meters are used to measure the resistance of a material's surface to the flow of electricity, providing insight into its electrical properties. On the other hand, volume resistivity meters are used to measure the resistance of a material throughout its volume, providing a more comprehensive analysis of its electrical conductivity. Both types of meters play a crucial role in various industries for testing and quality control purposes.</p></p>
