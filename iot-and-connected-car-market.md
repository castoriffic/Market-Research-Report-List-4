<p><p>The IoT (Internet of Things) and Connected Car market includes various types of communication networks such as V2V (Vehicle-to-Vehicle), V2I (Vehicle-to-Infrastructure), V2P (Vehicle-to-Pedestrian), and V2N (Vehicle-to-Network). V2V enables vehicles to communicate with each other to enhance safety and efficiency on the road. V2I allows vehicles to communicate with infrastructure like traffic lights and road signs. V2P facilitates communication between vehicles and pedestrians to prevent accidents. V2N involves vehicles connecting to cloud networks for data sharing and updates.</p></p>
