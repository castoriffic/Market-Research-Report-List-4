<p><p>PCB packaging materials market includes metal packages, plastic packages, and ceramic packages. Metal packages are durable and provide excellent electromagnetic shielding. Plastic packages are lightweight, cost-effective, and offer good thermal performance. Ceramic packages are highly reliable, provide excellent electrical insulation, and have high thermal conductivity. Each type of packaging material caters to different requirements in terms of durability, cost, thermal performance, and electrical insulation, allowing manufacturers to choose the most suitable option for their specific needs.</p></p>
