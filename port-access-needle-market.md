There are three main types of port access needles: winged port access needles, angled port access needles, and straight port access needles. Winged port access needles are designed with small wings for easy grip and stability. Angled port access needles have a curved design for improved visibility and access to ports at different angles. Straight port access needles are used for straightforward access to ports without any extra features.</p></p>
