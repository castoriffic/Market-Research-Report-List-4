<p><p>Heavy duty alternators are commonly categorized into two types: brush heavy duty alternators and brushless heavy duty alternators. Brush heavy duty alternators use brushes to conduct electrical current from the stationary to the rotating parts of the alternator, while brushless heavy duty alternators use a magnetic field to transfer power instead. Both types of alternators are widely used in heavy-duty machinery and vehicles such as trucks, buses, construction equipment, and agricultural machinery for efficient power generation.</p></p>
