<p><strong>The Aviation Carbon Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Fiber</li><li>Chopped Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous fiber and chopped fiber are two types of carbon fiber utilized in the aviation industry. Continuous fiber consists of long, continuous strands of carbon fiber that provide high strength and stiffness, making it suitable for structural components like wings and fuselage. On the other hand, chopped fiber consists of shorter, randomly oriented fibers mixed with a resin to create composite materials for non-structural parts like interior components. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and efficiency of aircraft materials.</p></p>
