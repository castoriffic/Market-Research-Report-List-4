Conductive coatings and paints contain metal particles that provide a conductive layer to shield against EMI. Metal shielding involves using metal enclosures or cages to block electromagnetic waves. Conductive polymers are materials with conductive properties that can be used for EMI shielding. EMI/EMC filters market includes products that reduce electromagnetic noise to meet regulatory standards.</p></p>
