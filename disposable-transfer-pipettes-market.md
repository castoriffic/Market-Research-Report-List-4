<p><p>Disposable transfer pipettes are commonly used in laboratories and medical settings for transferring small amounts of liquids. There are two main types in the market: disposable graduated transfer pipettes and disposable nongraduated transfer pipettes. Graduated transfer pipettes have markings to indicate the volume being transferred, making them suitable for precise measurements. On the other hand, nongraduated transfer pipettes do not have volume markings and are used for transferring liquids without the need for exact measurements. Both types offer convenience and hygiene benefits compared to reusable pipettes.</p></p>
