<p><ul><li>Normally Closed Type</li><li>Normally Open Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrothermic actuators can be classified into two types based on their working principle: Normally Closed Type and Normally Open Type. In Normally Closed Type, the actuator remains closed when no power is applied, and opens when power is turned on. On the other hand, Normally Open Type actuators remain open when no power is applied, and close when power is turned on. These two different types cater to different applications and requirements in the electrothermic actuator market.</p></p>
