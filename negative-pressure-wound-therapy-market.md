<p><p>Negative Pressure Wound Therapy (NPWT) is a technique used in the treatment of chronic and acute wounds by applying negative pressure to the wound site. This therapy helps in promoting wound healing by removing excess fluids, reducing edema, promoting blood flow, and facilitating granulation tissue formation. NPWT is commonly used in the management of surgical wounds, diabetic foot ulcers, pressure ulcers, and venous leg ulcers among other conditions.</p><p>The Negative Pressure Wound Therapy Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.50% during the forecast period. One of the key factors driving the growth of this market is the rising incidence of chronic wounds and ulcers due to factors such as diabetes, obesity, and an aging population.
