<p><strong>The Medical Surgical Sutures Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbable</li><li>Nonabsorbable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical Surgical Sutures market consists of two main types: Absorbable and Nonabsorbable sutures. Absorbable sutures are designed to break down in the body over time, eliminating the need for removal after the wound has healed. These sutures are typically made from materials like catgut, polyglycolic acid, and polyglactin. Nonabsorbable sutures, on the other hand, are permanent and must be removed after the wound has healed. These sutures are commonly made from materials like silk, nylon, and polypropylene.</p></p>
