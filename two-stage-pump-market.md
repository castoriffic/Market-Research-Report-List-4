<p><ul><li>Series Connection</li><li>Parallel Connection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the series connection market, two stage pumps are connected one after the other in a linear configuration. This setup increases the overall pressure output of the pump system. In the parallel connection market, two stage pumps are connected side by side, which increases the volume capacity of the system. Both types cater to different requirements, with series connection being more suitable for applications requiring higher pressure, while parallel connection is preferred for applications requiring higher flow rates.</p></p>
