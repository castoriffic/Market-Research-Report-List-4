<p><p>Picture Archiving and Communication Systems (PACS) market includes two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based PACS involves storing medical images and patient data on remote servers accessed through the internet, offering flexibility and scalability. On the other hand, on-premises PACS involves storing data locally within a healthcare facility, providing more control over security. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of healthcare providers, with cloud-based solutions gaining popularity due to their cost-effectiveness and ease of implementation.</p></p>
