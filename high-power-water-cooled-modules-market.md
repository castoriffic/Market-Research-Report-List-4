<p><p>High power water cooled modules market includes three main types: Dual Diode, Thyristor-Diode, and Thyristor-Thyristor modules. Dual Diode modules are comprised of two diodes in one package, ideal for applications requiring high efficiency and low power loss. Thyristor-Diode modules combine the functions of a thyristor and a diode, offering improved control over power flow. Thyristor-Thyristor modules consist of two thyristors, enabling precise regulation of high power loads. Each type serves specific purposes in industrial applications requiring reliable and efficient power control.</p></p>
