<p><p>Non-stick coatings market is segmented into PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene), PFA (Perfluoroalkoxy), and FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene) based on their chemical composition and properties. PTFE coatings are known for their high resistance to heat and chemical reactions, making them ideal for cookware. PFA coatings offer enhanced durability and stability at higher temperatures, suitable for industrial applications. FEP coatings have a lower melting point and are commonly used in non-stick tapes and linings. Each type of coating caters to specific needs across various industries.</p></p>
