<p><p>Extension cord reels are available in two main types: manual operation and automatic. Manual operation reels require the user to manually wind up the cord after use, whereas automatic reels use a spring-loaded mechanism to automatically retract the cord with the push of a button. Manual operation reels are more affordable and widely available, while automatic reels offer convenience and ease of use. Both types are popular choices for organizing and storing extension cords in a neat and efficient manner.</p></p>
