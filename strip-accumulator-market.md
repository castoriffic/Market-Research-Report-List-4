<p><strong>The Strip Accumulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Strip Accumulator</li><li>Horizontal Strip Accumulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Strip accumulators are used in industries to store and accumulate strip material. Vertical strip accumulators stack the strips vertically, allowing for easy feeding of material into the production line. Horizontal strip accumulators store the strips horizontally, making it easier to feed the material at a different angle. Both types of accumulators are used based on the production requirements and the layout of the production line. Vertical strip accumulators are ideal for limited space while horizontal strip accumulators are suitable for longer strips.</p></p>
