Lead Oxide(II) is the most common type used in lead-acid batteries. Lead Oxide(II, IV) is a mix of lead oxide in different oxidation states and is utilized in some specialized battery applications. Lead Oxide(IV) is a high-performance variant suitable for demanding battery applications requiring superior efficiency and longer lifespan. Each type offers varying levels of performance and suitability for different battery requirements.</p></p>
