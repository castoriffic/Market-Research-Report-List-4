<p><p>The Silicone Rubber Waterproof Sealant market can be divided into different types based on the curing agents used. Acetic Acid Sealant, also known as acid-curing sealant, offers strong adhesion and durability. Alcohol Sealant, on the other hand, is solvent-based and provides flexibility and resistance to extreme temperatures. Amine Sealant, a type of neutral curing sealant, is recommended for applications where odors and corrosive effects are a concern. Each type has its own strengths and is suitable for various sealing applications.</p></p>
