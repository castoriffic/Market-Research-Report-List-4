<p><p>Soluble Soybean Polysaccharides (SSPS) are classified into two market types based on their viscosity levels. Type B, or Medium Viscosity SSPS, have a thicker consistency compared to Type A, or Low Viscosity SSPS. Type B is commonly used in food products as a stabilizer, thickener, or emulsifier, providing a smooth texture. On the other hand, Type A is preferred for its easy dispersibility and ability to form clear solutions, making it suitable for beverages and other liquid applications.</p></p>
