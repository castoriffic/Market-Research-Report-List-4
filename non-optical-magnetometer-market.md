<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Non Optical Magnetometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Magnetometer</li><li>Relative Magnetometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non Optical Magnetometer market is segmented into two main types: Absolute Magnetometer and Relative Magnetometer. Absolute Magnetometer measures the absolute value of the magnetic field regardless of its direction, making it suitable for applications requiring high accuracy such as geophysical surveys. On the other hand, Relative Magnetometer measures the changes in the magnetic field relative to a reference point, making it more suitable for applications where the direction of the magnetic field is not critical, such as in consumer electronics.</p></p>
