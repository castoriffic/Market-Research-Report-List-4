<p><p>Hemodialyzer market types can be categorized into High Throughput and Low Throughput systems. High Throughput hemodialyzers are designed for hospitals or dialysis centers with a large number of patients, allowing for swift and efficient processing of multiple patients at once. On the other hand, Low Throughput hemodialyzers are suitable for smaller facilities or for home use, catering to a lower volume of patients. Both types aim to provide effective renal replacement therapy, but differ in scalability and processing capabilities.</p></p>
