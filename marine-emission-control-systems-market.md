<p><p>Marine emission control systems market includes various types of technologies to reduce emissions from ships. Scrubber systems use water to clean exhaust gases, while Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) systems recirculate a portion of exhaust gas back into the engine for combustion. Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) systems reduce nitrogen oxide emissions by converting them into harmless nitrogen and water. Other technologies in this market include fuel emulsification and oxidation catalysts, all aimed at reducing environmental impact of marine transportation.</p></p>
