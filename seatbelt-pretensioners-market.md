<p><p>Seatbelt pretensioners are safety features in vehicles that tighten the seatbelt in the event of a collision to reduce the movement of the occupant and minimize injuries. There are three main types of seatbelt pretensioners: lap pretensioners, buckle pretensioners, and retractor pretensioners. Lap pretensioners tighten the lap portion of the seatbelt, buckle pretensioners tighten the buckle end of the seatbelt, and retractor pretensioners tighten the entire seatbelt. Each type plays a vital role in ensuring the safety of vehicle occupants in the event of a crash.</p></p>
