<p><p>Pneumatic Direct-acting Solenoid Valves are categorized into different types based on the number of ways they control the flow of air. Three-way SV valves have three ports and can switch between two different paths of airflow. Four-way SV valves have four ports and can control the direction of airflow for two different air circuits. Five-way SV valves have five ports and can control the flow of air to two different output ports. Other types of valves may have different configurations or functions based on specific application requirements.</p></p>
