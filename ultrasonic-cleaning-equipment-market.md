<p><p>Ultrasonic cleaning equipment utilizes different types of technologies, with two main types being magnetostrictive and piezoelectric. Magnetostrictive technology uses a magnetic field to induce ultrasonic vibrations in a metal rod, while piezoelectric technology uses crystals that change shape when an electric current is applied, creating ultrasonic vibrations. Both types are commonly used in industrial and commercial settings for cleaning a variety of objects and materials. These technologies offer efficient and effective cleaning processes for various applications.</p></p>
