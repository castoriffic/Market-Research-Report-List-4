<p><p>The semi-automatic vacuum packaging machine market consists of two main types - vertical form fill seal (V-FFS) and horizontal form fill seal (H-FFS) machines. V-FFS machines are designed to form the package vertically and fill it with the product before sealing it, while H-FFS machines do the same process horizontally. Both types of machines are commonly used in the food industry for packaging products such as meat, cheese, fruits, and vegetables to extend their shelf life and maintain freshness.</p></p>
