<p><p>Endoscopic Forceps are instruments used during minimally invasive procedures to grasp, manipulate, or cut tissues. There are two main types: Monopolar Endoscopic Forceps, which use electrical current to cauterize tissues, and Bipolar Endoscopic Forceps, which have two branches that both deliver and return electrical current, allowing for more precise tissue cutting without damaging surrounding areas. Both types are essential tools in various endoscopic procedures, providing surgeons with increased dexterity and control.</p></p>
