<p><p>Ultra Heat Treatment (UHT) System is used for heating liquid food to extremely high temperatures to ensure longer shelf life without refrigeration. There are two main types of UHT systems - Direct Heating UHT System and Indirect Heating UHT System. In the Direct Heating system, liquid is heated directly by passing through a steam injector or by direct steam injection. In the Indirect Heating system, liquid is heated by indirect heat exchange with steam or hot water in a heat exchanger before reaching the UHT treatment.</p></p>
