<p><p>Sealed switches are available in different types to suit various applications. SPDT switches, also known as Single Pole Double Throw, have three terminals and can control two separate circuits simultaneously. SPST-NC switches, or Single Pole Single Throw Normally Closed, are normally closed and open when activated. SPST-NO switches, or Single Pole Single Throw Normally Open, are normally open and close when activated. These different types cater to different needs and requirements in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics.</p></p>
