<p><p>Robot welding systems are classified into three main types - arc welding, spot welding, and others. Arc welding involves using an electric current to create an arc between an electrode and the base material to melt and join metals. Spot welding involves applying pressure and heat to two metal surfaces to join them together. Other types of welding processes include laser welding, friction welding, and plasma welding, each offering unique advantages for different applications and industries.</p></p>
