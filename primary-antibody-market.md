<p><p>Primary antibody market is divided into two main types: Monoclonal Antibodies and Polyclonal Antibodies. Monoclonal antibodies are created from identical immune cells and target specific antigens, making them highly specific and consistent in their performance. On the other hand, polyclonal antibodies are generated from multiple immune cells and recognize different epitopes on an antigen, providing a broader range of detection. Both types play a crucial role in research, diagnostics, and therapeutics in the field of immunology.</p></p>
