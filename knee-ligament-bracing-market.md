<p><p>Knee ligament bracing market consists of two main types: internal support and external support. Internal support braces are worn inside the knee and provide stability by compressing the joint and supporting the ligaments from within. External support braces, on the other hand, are worn on the outside of the knee and provide additional stability and protection by restricting movement and absorbing impact. Both types of braces are designed to help prevent injuries and provide support for individuals with knee ligament issues.</p></p>
