<p><p>Bio-implants come in various types, such as allografts, autografts, and xenografts. Allografts are implants sourced from a different individual of the same species, autografts are implants sourced from the same individual, and xenografts are implants sourced from a different species. Each type of implant has its own benefits and limitations, depending on factors like compatibility and risk of rejection. These bio-implants play a crucial role in medical procedures, including bone grafts, skin grafts, and organ transplants.</p></p>
