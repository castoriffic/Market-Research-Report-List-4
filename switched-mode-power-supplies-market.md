<p><p>Switched Mode Power Supplies, also known as SMPS, incorporate different types of converters to efficiently convert and regulate electrical power. The DC-DC Converter type is designed to convert one DC voltage to another DC voltage. Forward Converter converts input voltage to a higher output voltage. Flyback Converter is suitable for low power applications, providing galvanic isolation between input and output. Self-oscillating Flyback Converter offers simplicity and versatility for various power levels. Each type serves specific purposes in delivering regulated power efficiently and effectively.</p></p>
