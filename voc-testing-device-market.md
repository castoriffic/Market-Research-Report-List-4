<p><p>VOC Testing Devices are used to measure volatile organic compounds in the air. There are two main types of devices used in the market: PID (Photoionization Detector) and Metal-oxide Semiconductor. PID devices work by ionizing gas molecules and measuring the resulting current, while Metal-oxide Semiconductor devices use a heated metal oxide to react with VOCs and produce a change in resistance. Both types of devices are commonly used in industrial settings, environmental monitoring, and indoor air quality testing.</p></p>
