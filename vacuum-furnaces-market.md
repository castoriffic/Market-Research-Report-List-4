<p><p>Vacuum furnaces are used for various heat-treating processes, including quenching, brazing, and carburizing. Vacuum quench furnaces are designed to rapidly cool materials to increase their hardness and strength. Vacuum brazing furnaces are used to join materials at high temperatures in a controlled environment. Vacuum carburizing furnaces are used to introduce carbon into the surface of materials to improve their wear resistance. These different types of vacuum furnaces cater to specific heat-treating needs in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and tool manufacturing.</p></p>
