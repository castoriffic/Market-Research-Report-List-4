<p><p>High Temperature Vacuum Sintering Furnace market can be classified into two types based on the reclaiming method used: Unilateral Reclaiming Method and Bilateral Reclaiming Method. Unilateral reclaiming method involves reclaiming materials only from one side of the furnace, whereas bilateral reclaiming method allows for materials to be replenished from both sides of the furnace. These methods impact the efficiency and cost-effectiveness of the sintering process, making them important considerations for manufacturers when choosing a sintering furnace.</p></p>
