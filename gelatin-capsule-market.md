<p><p>Gelatin capsules are classified into various types based on their intended use and properties. Stomach-soluble gelatin capsules are designed to dissolve in the stomach, releasing the contents quickly. Enterosoluble vacant gelatin capsules are resistant to stomach acids and only dissolve in the intestines. Other markets for gelatin capsules include delayed-release capsules, which are designed to release their contents at a specific time or location in the digestive tract, and flavored capsules for easier consumption. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industries.</p></p>
