<p><p>360 Degree Imaging sonars provide a full panoramic view around the system, allowing for comprehensive coverage of the underwater environment. Image Down sonars focus on generating detailed images directly below the sonar, providing enhanced visibility for objects beneath the boat. Lateral Imaging sonars produce side-to-side images of the underwater surroundings, aiding in navigation and obstacle avoidance. Other types of sonars may include specialty systems designed for specific applications, such as forward-looking sonars for detecting obstacles ahead of the vessel.</p></p>
