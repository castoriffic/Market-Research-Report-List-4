Tubed fluid coils are made up of a series of tubes through which the fluid flows, while finned fluid coils have fins attached to the tubes to increase the surface area for better heat transfer. Both types of fluid coils are used in various applications to efficiently transfer heat between the fluid and the surrounding air or water.</p></p>
