<p><ul><li>Amplifying Stethoscope</li><li>Digitizing Stethoscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical electronic stethoscopes market includes two main types: amplifying stethoscopes and digitizing stethoscopes. Amplifying stethoscopes are equipped with built-in amplifiers that enhance the sound of the patient's heartbeat or breath sounds, making it easier for healthcare professionals to auscultate. Digitizing stethoscopes convert the analog heart sounds into digital signals, allowing for better visualization and recording of the sounds. Both types of electronic stethoscopes offer advanced features that aid in accurate diagnosis and monitoring of patients' conditions.</p></p>
