<p><p>Military aircraft weighing platforms can be categorized into two types: digital and analog. Digital weighing platforms utilize electronic sensors to measure the weight of the aircraft with high accuracy and precision. These platforms often have advanced features such as data recording and automatic calculations. On the other hand, analog weighing platforms use mechanical systems like load cells and springs to measure the weight of the aircraft. While they may not offer the same level of accuracy as digital platforms, analog platforms are often more cost-effective and simpler to operate.</p></p>
