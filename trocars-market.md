<p><p>Trocars are medical devices used in minimally invasive surgeries to create access ports for other surgical instruments. There are two main types of Trocars- Cutting Trocars and Dilating Trocars. Cutting Trocars have a sharp blade for incisions, while Dilating Trocars have a blunt tip that stretches the tissue as it enters, reducing the risk of cutting or damaging surrounding structures. Both types of Trocars play important roles in providing safe and effective access during minimally invasive procedures.</p></p>
