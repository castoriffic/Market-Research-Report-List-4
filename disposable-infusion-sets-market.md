<p><ul><li>Pump Infusion Set</li><li>Non-pump Infusion Set</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Disposable infusion sets are divided into two main types: pump infusion sets and non-pump infusion sets. Pump infusion sets are designed to work with infusion pumps to deliver a controlled amount of medication or fluids to the patient. These sets are typically used for continuous infusion therapy. Non-pump infusion sets do not require a pump and are manually controlled by healthcare professionals. They are commonly used for intermittent infusion therapy. Both types of infusion sets are disposable, ensuring safe and sterile delivery of fluids to the patient.</p></p>
