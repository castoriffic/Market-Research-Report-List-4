Horizontal Axis Type Rotavators have a rotating shaft along the horizontal axis, which helps in breaking up soil and mixing crop residues effectively. On the other hand, Vertical Axis Type Rotavators have a rotating shaft along the vertical axis, providing better soil pulverization and mixing. Each type of Rotavator caters to specific farming needs and conditions, offering farmers a choice based on their requirements.</p></p>
