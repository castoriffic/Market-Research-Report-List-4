<p><ul><li>Soil-less</li><li>Soil-based​</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Green walls can be categorized into two main types: soil-less and soil-based systems. Soil-less green walls utilize alternative growing mediums such as felt or foam to support plant growth, making them lightweight and easier to install indoors. On the other hand, soil-based green walls use traditional soil as a growing medium, providing a more natural environment for plants and allowing for a wider variety of species to be grown. Both types offer a visually appealing and sustainable solution for incorporating greenery into urban spaces.</p></p>
