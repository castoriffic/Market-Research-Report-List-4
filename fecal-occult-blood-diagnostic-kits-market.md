<p><p>Fecal Occult Blood Diagnostic Kits are used to detect hidden blood in stool samples, which can indicate various gastrointestinal conditions such as colorectal cancer. There are two main types of these kits: Immunochemical Fecal Occult Blood Test (iFOBT or FIT) and Guaiac Fecal Occult Blood Test (gFOBT). iFOBT is more sensitive and specific than gFOBT, as it detects human blood globin, while gFOBT detects peroxidase activity from both human and non-human sources. FIT kits are becoming more popular due to their accuracy and ease of use.</p></p>
