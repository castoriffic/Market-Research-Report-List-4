<p><p>Stationary cone crushers can be categorized into two main types: Fixed crushers and mobile crushers. Fixed crushers are permanently installed at a specific location and are best suited for large mining and construction projects requiring constant use. On the other hand, mobile crushers are designed to be easily moved between job sites, making them ideal for smaller projects and road construction. Both types offer efficient crushing capabilities, but the choice between fixed and mobile depends on the specific needs of the project.</p></p>
