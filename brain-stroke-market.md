<p><p>A brain stroke, also known as a cerebrovascular accident, occurs when blood flow to a part of the brain is disrupted as a result of a blocked artery or a ruptured blood vessel. This disruption deprives the brain of oxygen and nutrients, leading to brain cell damage and potentially life-threatening consequences. Symptoms of a stroke can include sudden numbness or weakness in the face, arm, or leg, confusion, trouble speaking or understanding speech, sudden trouble seeing in one or both eyes, dizziness, loss of balance, and severe headache.</p><p>The Brain Stroke Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.8% during the forecast period.
