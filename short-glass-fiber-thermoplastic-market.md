<p><p>Short glass fiber thermoplastic is commonly used in two main market types: injection molding and extrusion molding. </p><p>Injection molding involves the process of injecting molten material into a mold cavity, which then cools and hardens into a specific shape. The short glass fibers help reinforce the material and improve its strength, making it ideal for products that require durability. </p><p>On the other hand, extrusion molding is a process where the material is forced through a die to create a continuous shape. Short glass fiber thermoplastics are often used in this market for applications such as pipes, tubing, and sheeting.</p></p>
