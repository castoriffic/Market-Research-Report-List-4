<p><p>High Frequency Inductors are available in various types including wire wound type, film type, and multilayer type. Wire wound type inductors are made by winding a conducting wire around a core, providing high inductance values. Film type inductors utilize a thin film coating on a core material to achieve high frequency performance. Multilayer type inductors consist of multiple layers of conducting material stacked on top of each other, offering compact size and high efficiency. Each type caters to different applications and requirements in the high frequency market.</p></p>
