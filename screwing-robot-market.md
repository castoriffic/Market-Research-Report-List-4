<p><p>Screwing robots can be classified into two main market types based on their functioning mechanism: Air Suction Type and Air Blow Type. Air Suction Type robots use negative pressure to pick up screws and securely place them, while Air Blow Type robots use compressed air to blow screws into position. Both types offer efficient and precise screwing capabilities, with the main difference lying in the method used to handle and place the screws. Each type caters to different industrial applications depending on the specific screwing requirements.</p></p>
