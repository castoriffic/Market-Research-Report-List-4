<p><p>Granulated Honey and Powdered Honey are two types of Honey Powder available in the market. Powdered Honey, on the other hand, has been further processed to create a fine powder that can easily dissolve in liquids and mix well in dry ingredients. Both types of Honey Powder provide the sweet taste and health benefits of honey in a convenient, easy-to-use form.</p></p>
