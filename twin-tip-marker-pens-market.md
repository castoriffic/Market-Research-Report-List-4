<p><p>Twin-tip marker pens come in two main types: Permanent and Non-Permanent. Permanent markers are designed to create long-lasting, fade-resistant marks on surfaces such as paper, plastic, and metal. They are ideal for labeling items and making permanent notes. Non-Permanent markers, on the other hand, are suitable for temporary markings that can be easily wiped away or erased. They are commonly used for writing on whiteboards, glass, and other smooth surfaces where the markings need to be removed.</p></p>
