<p><p>Static retorts are commonly used in food processing industries for sterilizing food products in sealed containers. There are three main types of static retorts: Steam and Water Spray Retort, Water Immersion Retort, and Air Steam Retort. Steam and Water Spray Retorts use a combination of steam and water spray to heat the products evenly. Water Immersion Retorts involve submerging the containers in hot water for sterilization. Air Steam Retorts use a combination of hot air and steam for heating the products.</p></p>
