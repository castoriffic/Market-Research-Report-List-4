Whole-Genome Sequencing analyzes the entire DNA sequence of an individual, providing comprehensive genetic information. On the other hand, Whole Exome Sequencing focuses specifically on the coding regions of genes, which make up only a small fraction of the genome. Both services have unique advantages and applications in research, clinical diagnostics, and personalized medicine, catering to diverse needs in the genomics market.</p></p>
