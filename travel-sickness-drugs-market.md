<p><ul><li>Anticholinergic</li><li>Antihistamines</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The travel sickness drugs market can be classified into three main types: anticholinergic, antihistamines, and others. Anticholinergic drugs work by blocking the action of acetylcholine, a neurotransmitter that affects motion sickness. Antihistamines, on the other hand, block histamine receptors in the brain, reducing symptoms of nausea and vomiting. The "others" category includes alternative treatments such as ginger supplements or prescription medications like scopolamine patches. Each type of drug targets different pathways in the body to alleviate symptoms of travel sickness.</p></p>
