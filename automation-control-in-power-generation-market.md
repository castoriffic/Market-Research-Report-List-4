<p><p>Automation control plays a crucial role in the power generation market, with various types of control systems being utilized. Distributed Control System (DCS) allows for decentralized control of individual plant processes. Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) systems monitor and control entire power plants. Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs) are used for real-time control of equipment. Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES) help in managing manufacturing processes, including scheduling and tracking of production. These systems ensure efficient and reliable operation of power generation facilities.</p></p>
