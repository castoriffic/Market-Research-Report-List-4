<p><p>Organic bread flour for home cooking can be categorized into two main market types: machine milled flour and stone-ground flour. Machine milled flour is produced using high-speed steel rollers to grind the wheat, resulting in a fine texture which is often preferred for commercial baking. On the other hand, stone-ground flour is made by grinding wheat between two large stones, preserving more of the grain's flavor and nutrients. Both types of flour are popular choices for home bakers looking for high-quality organic ingredients.</p></p>
