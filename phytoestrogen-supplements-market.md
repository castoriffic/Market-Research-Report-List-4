<p><p>Phytoestrogen supplements are categorized into two main types: Flavonoids and Non-Flavonoids. Flavonoids are compounds found in plants like soybeans, red clover, and flaxseed that mimic estrogen in the body. They are believed to have potential benefits for menopausal symptoms and bone health. Non-Flavonoids, on the other hand, include lignans and coumestans which also have estrogen-like effects. Both types of phytoestrogen supplements are available in the market and are popular choices for individuals seeking natural alternatives to hormone replacement therapy.</p></p>
