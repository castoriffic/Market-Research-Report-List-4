<p><p>Self-adhesive labels are widely used in various industries for branding and product identification. The Flexographic Printing market for self-adhesive labels utilizes a flexible relief plate to transfer ink onto the label material. This method is ideal for high-volume printing at a low cost. On the other hand, Letterpress Printing market for self-adhesive labels involves a raised surface plate that transfers ink directly onto the label material, providing a high-quality, textured finish. Both printing methods offer versatility and customization options for label production.</p></p>
