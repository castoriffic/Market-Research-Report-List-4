<p><ul><li>Surface Area Analyzer</li><li>Pore Size Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Physisorption analyzers are used to analyze the surface area and pore size in materials. Surface area analyzers measure the total surface area of a material by adsorbing gas molecules onto the surface and measuring the amount adsorbed. Pore size analyzers measure the size and distribution of pores in a material by analyzing the adsorption and desorption of gases. Both types of analyzers are essential tools for researchers and industries working with materials such as catalysts, adsorbents, and membranes.</p></p>
