Patient warming systems help maintain normal body temperature during surgical procedures by using devices such as warming blankets or forced-air warming systems. On the other hand, patient cooling systems are used to induce therapeutic hypothermia for patients suffering from conditions like cardiac arrest or traumatic brain injury. Both types of systems play a crucial role in regulating patient temperature and aiding in recovery.</p></p>
