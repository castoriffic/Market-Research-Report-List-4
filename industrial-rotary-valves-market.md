<p><p>Industrial rotary valves are commonly categorized into three main types: butterfly valves, ball valves, and plug valves. Butterfly valves control flow using a disk with a rotating shaft and are ideal for large flow applications. Ball valves use a spherical closure to regulate flow and are known for their reliability and durability. Plug valves utilize cylindrical or conical plugs to control flow and provide a tight seal. Each type of valve has unique characteristics that make them suitable for different industrial applications.</p></p>
