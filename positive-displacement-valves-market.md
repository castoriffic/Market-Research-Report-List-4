<p><p>Positive Displacement Valves are categorized into two main types: Pneumatic Positive Displacement Valve and Electric Positive Displacement Valve markets. Pneumatic valves operate using compressed air or gas to control the flow of fluid, while electric valves use electrical signals to open or close the valve. Both types offer precise control and high reliability in various industrial applications. Pneumatic valves are preferred for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness, while electric valves are chosen for their automation capabilities and ease of integration with control systems.</p></p>
