<p><p>Piezoelectric Ceramic Sensors are available in various types to suit different applications. Globe sensors are shaped like a sphere and provide omnidirectional sensing capabilities. Tube sensors are cylindrical in shape and ideal for applications requiring linear sensing. Disc sensors are flat and provide uniform sensing over a larger area. Ring sensors are donut-shaped and offer precise radial sensing. Stick sensors are long and slender, suitable for applications requiring extended reach. Bending piece sensors are flexible and ideal for detecting subtle changes in pressure or vibration.</p></p>
