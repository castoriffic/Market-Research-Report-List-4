<p><p>Microwave occupancy sensors are available in two main types: Ceiling-mounted and Wall-mounted. Ceiling-mounted sensors are installed on the ceiling of a room and provide a wide coverage area, making them ideal for large spaces such as conference rooms or open-plan offices. Wall-mounted sensors, on the other hand, are installed on the walls and are more suitable for smaller areas like restrooms or hallways. Both types utilize microwave technology to detect motion and occupancy, providing energy savings and convenience in lighting control.</p></p>
