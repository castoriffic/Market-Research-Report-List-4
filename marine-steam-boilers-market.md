<p><p>Marine steam boilers are classified into various types based on their structural design and operational characteristics. Smoke tube boilers utilize smoke and heat to generate steam. Water tube boilers have water flowing inside the tubes to create steam. Internally fired boilers have combustion chambers within the boiler shell, while externally fired boilers have combustion chambers outside the shell. Low pressure boilers operate at pressures below 15 psi, while high pressure boilers operate at pressures above 15 psi. Each type caters to different marine industry needs and requirements.</p></p>
