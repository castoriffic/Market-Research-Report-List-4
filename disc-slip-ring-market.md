<p><p>Disc slip rings are categorized into two types: integrated disc slip rings and separate disc slip rings. Integrated disc slip rings are designed with the disc and slip rings combined into a single unit, offering a more compact and simplified solution for transmitting power and signals. On the other hand, separate disc slip rings consist of a separate disc and slip ring component, providing flexibility in installation and maintenance. Both types of disc slip rings cater to different needs and requirements in various industries where rotational movement is required.</p></p>
