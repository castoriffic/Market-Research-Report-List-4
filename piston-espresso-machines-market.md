<p><p>Piston espresso machines come in three market types: manually operated, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manually operated machines require the user to control the extraction process, offering a hands-on and customizable experience. Semi-automatic machines automate the water delivery, but still require manual control over grind size and extraction time. Fully automatic machines handle the entire brewing process with the push of a button, making them the most convenient option for busy users. Each type caters to different preferences and levels of involvement in the espresso-making process.</p></p>
