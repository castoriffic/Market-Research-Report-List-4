<p><strong>The PPLN Waveguide Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Proton in-diffused (RPE) Waveguides</li><li>Ridge-Waveguides</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PPLN waveguide market includes Proton in-diffused (RPE) Waveguides and Ridge-Waveguides. Proton in-diffused (RPE) Waveguides involve the diffusion of protons into a Lithium Niobate crystal to create the waveguide structure. These waveguides offer high efficiency and low insertion loss. Ridge-Waveguides, on the other hand, are formed by etching a ridge structure on a Lithium Niobate substrate. They provide precise control over the waveguide dimensions, leading to improved performance in terms of confinement and propagation of light.</p></p>
