<p><p>Infant resuscitators are devices used to provide breathing support for newborns in emergency situations. There are two main types of infant resuscitators: self-inflating resuscitators and flow-inflating resuscitators. Self-inflating resuscitators have a bag that fills with oxygen when squeezed, while flow-inflating resuscitators deliver a controlled flow of oxygen to the infant. Both types of resuscitators are essential in neonatal care to ensure proper oxygenation and ventilation for newborns in distress.</p></p>
