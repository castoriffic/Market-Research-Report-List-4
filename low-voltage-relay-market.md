<p><strong>The Low Voltage Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Voltage Relay</li><li>Static Voltage Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low voltage relays can be categorized into two types: electromagnetic voltage relay and static voltage relay. Electromagnetic voltage relays use magnetic coils to switch contacts in response to changes in voltage, making them suitable for applications requiring high sensitivity and accuracy. On the other hand, static voltage relays use solid-state components to control the switching of contacts, offering more reliability and durability. Both types cater to different needs in the market for low voltage relay solutions.</p></p>
