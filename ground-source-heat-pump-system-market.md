<p><ul><li>Underground Tube Ground Source Heat Pump System</li><li>Groundwater Source Heat Pump System</li><li>Surface Water Ground Source Heat Pump System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ground-source heat pump systems are classified into three main types based on the heat exchange method: Underground Tube Ground Source Heat Pump System, Groundwater Source Heat Pump System, and Surface Water Ground Source Heat Pump System. The Underground Tube system utilizes buried pipes to exchange heat with the ground, the Groundwater system uses wells to extract heat from underground water sources, and the Surface Water system involves extracting heat from bodies of water such as rivers or lakes. Each type offers unique advantages and is suitable for different geographical locations and conditions.</p></p>
