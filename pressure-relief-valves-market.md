<p><p>Pressure Relief Valves are crucial for maintaining safe operating conditions in various industries. Direct Acting Pressure Relief Valves operate by relieving excess pressure directly through a spring-loaded mechanism. Pilot Operated Pressure Relief Valves use a pilot valve to control the opening and closing of the main relief valve. Other types of Pressure Relief Valves include balanced bellows relief valves, thermal relief valves, and rupture discs. Each type serves a specific purpose in protecting equipment and personnel from overpressurization incidents, ensuring smooth and safe operations.</p></p>
