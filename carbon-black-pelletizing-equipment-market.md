<p><ul><li>Wet Pelletizing</li><li>Dry Pelletizing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carbon black pelletizing equipment market can be categorized into two main types - wet pelletizing and dry pelletizing. Wet pelletizing involves adding a liquid binder to the carbon black powder to form pellets, while dry pelletizing uses pressure and heat to compact the powder into pellets without the need for a liquid binder. Both methods have their own advantages and disadvantages, with wet pelletizing generally being more cost-effective but requiring additional drying processes, while dry pelletizing is faster and more energy-efficient.</p></p>
