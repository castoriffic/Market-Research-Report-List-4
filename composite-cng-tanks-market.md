<p><p>Composite CNG tanks are used to store compressed natural gas in vehicles. The market for these tanks can be classified into two main types: glass fiber composites and carbon fiber composites. Glass fiber composites are made from glass strands embedded in a resin matrix, providing a cost-effective option with good strength and durability. On the other hand, carbon fiber composites are lighter and stronger than glass fiber composites, making them ideal for high-performance applications where weight reduction is important.</p></p>
