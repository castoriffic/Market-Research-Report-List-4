<p><p>The Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) Devices market consists of two main types: Digital PCR and Real-time PCR. Digital PCR involves partitioning a sample into multiple individual reactions, which allows for increased sensitivity and accuracy in quantifying nucleic acids. Real-time PCR, on the other hand, enables the continuous monitoring of the amplification of DNA during the PCR process. Both types of PCR devices are widely used in research, diagnostics, and forensics for their speed, accuracy, and versatility in amplifying and quantifying DNA.</p></p>
