<p><p>Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) is a method used to evaluate the properties of materials, components, or systems without causing damage. There are various types of NDT methods, including Ultrasonic, Radiography, Liquid Penetrant, and Magnetic testing. Ultrasonic testing uses high-frequency sound waves to detect internal flaws, Radiography uses X-rays or gamma rays to inspect the internal structure, Liquid Penetrant testing uses dye to identify surface defects, and Magnetic testing uses magnetic fields to detect flaws in ferrous materials. Each method plays a crucial role in ensuring the integrity of structures and components.</p></p>
