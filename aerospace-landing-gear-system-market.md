<p><p>Aerospace Landing Gear System market consists of various types such as Strut Landing Gear, Rocker Landing Gear, Pontoon Landing Gear, and Framed Landing Gear. Strut Landing Gear is a type of landing gear with a single or multiple struts supporting the weight of the aircraft. Rocker Landing Gear consists of a single strut attached to a pivot point for smoother landing. Pontoon Landing Gear is designed for amphibious aircraft, featuring large pontoons for water landings. Framed Landing Gear is a traditional type with a framework supporting the weight of the aircraft on the ground.</p></p>
