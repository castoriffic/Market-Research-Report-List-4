<p><p>Catalytic converters are devices used in vehicles to reduce harmful emissions. The market for these converters is primarily divided into four types based on the materials used: Platinum, Palladium, Rhodium, and Other. Platinum converters are commonly used in gasoline engines, while Palladium converters are preferred for diesel engines. Rhodium converters are typically used in three-way catalytic converters. The "Other" market includes converters made from cheaper materials such as ceramic or iron. Each type of converter has its own benefits and applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
