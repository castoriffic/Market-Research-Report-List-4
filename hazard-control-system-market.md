Motors are used to power machinery, while servo valves regulate the flow of fluids. Sensors detect potential hazards, actuators respond to signals, and drives control the speed and direction of motors. These components work together to monitor and manage potential dangers in industrial environments, minimizing the risk of accidents and ensuring smooth operations.</p></p>
