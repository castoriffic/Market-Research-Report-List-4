<p><ul><li>Water-Cooled Condenser</li><li>Air-Cooled Condenser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Package air conditioners are classified into two main types based on their condenser: Water-cooled condenser and air-cooled condenser. Water-cooled condenser package air conditioners use water to remove heat from the refrigerant, making them more efficient but requiring a water source for operation. On the other hand, air-cooled condenser package air conditioners use air to cool down the refrigerant, making them easier to install and maintain but less efficient compared to water-cooled condensers. Both types cater to different needs and requirements in the market.</p></p>
