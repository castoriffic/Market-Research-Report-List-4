<p><strong>The Encapsulated Flavors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluid Bed Coating</li><li>Spray Drying</li><li>Glass Encapsulation</li><li>Spray Chilling/Congealing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Encapsulated flavors market types include fluid bed coating, spray drying, glass encapsulation, and spray chilling/congealing. The fluid bed coating method involves applying a thin layer of coating material onto the flavor particles. Spray drying involves creating a dry powder through atomization of liquid flavor emulsion. Glass encapsulation involves encapsulating the flavor in a glassy matrix to protect it from external factors. Spray chilling/congealing involves solidifying the flavor emulsion into spherical beads through rapid cooling. Each method offers unique benefits and applications in the food industry.</p></p>
