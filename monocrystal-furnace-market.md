<p><p>Monocrystal Furnace Market includes various types of furnaces, such as Lifting Furnace and Floating Zone Furnace. Lifting Furnace is designed with a mechanism to lift the crystal from the melt, allowing for precise control over the crystal growth process. On the other hand, Floating Zone Furnace suspends the crystal in the melt without any support, resulting in high-purity single crystals. Both types cater to different requirements in the production of high-quality monocrystalline materials for various industries.</p></p>
