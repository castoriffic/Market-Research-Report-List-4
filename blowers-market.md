<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Blower</li><li>Positive-Displacement Blower</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blowers market consists of three main types: Centrifugal Blowers, Positive-Displacement Blowers, and Other market. Centrifugal blowers use high-speed rotating impellers to increase the velocity of air or gas, making them ideal for applications requiring high airflow and low pressure. Positive-Displacement blowers, on the other hand, use rotating lobes to trap and transport air or gas, making them suitable for applications requiring high-pressure and low airflow. The Other market comprises various specialty blowers designed for specific industrial applications, such as regenerative blowers and multistage blowers.</p></p>
