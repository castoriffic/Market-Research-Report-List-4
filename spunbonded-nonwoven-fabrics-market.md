<p><p>Spunbonded nonwoven fabrics are commonly made from three main types of materials: polypropylene (PP), polyester (PET), and polyethylene (PE). </p><p>PP spunbonded nonwovens are lightweight and have good strength, making them ideal for disposable products such as medical gowns and masks. PET spunbonded nonwovens have higher abrasion resistance and are used in applications like geotextiles and filtration. PE spunbonded nonwovens are water-resistant and are often used in agriculture and packaging. Each material type has specific properties that make them suitable for different applications in various industries.</p></p>
