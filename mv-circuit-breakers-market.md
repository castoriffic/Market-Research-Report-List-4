<p><p>MV Circuit Breakers market includes three main types: Miniature Circuit Breaker (MCB), Moulded Case Circuit Breaker (MCCB), and Air Circuit Breaker (ACB). MCBs are used for low voltage applications, typically in residential and commercial buildings. MCCBs are used for higher voltage applications, such as industrial settings. ACBs are typically used for medium voltage applications, providing protection against overloads and short circuits. Each type of circuit breaker serves a specific purpose in electrical distribution systems, ensuring safety and reliability.</p></p>
