<p><p>Influenza medication market offers various types of drugs to treat the flu, such as Zanamivir, Oseltamivir, Peramivir, Amantadine, Rimantadine, and Inosine. Zanamivir, Oseltamivir, and Peramivir are neuraminidase inhibitors that work by blocking the activity of the influenza virus. Amantadine and Rimantadine are adamantanes that interfere with the virus's ability to replicate. Inosine is a nucleoside that helps boost the immune system's response to the flu virus. Each type of medication offers a different mechanism of action to combat influenza infections.</p></p>
