<p><p>Electrical Submersible Pumps (ESP) are commonly used in both Openwell and Borewell markets for pumping water. Openwell pumps are installed above ground level in a well or sump, drawing water from an open water source. Borewell pumps, on the other hand, are installed deep inside a borewell, extracting water from underground sources. Both types of pumps are crucial in providing efficient water supply for agricultural, industrial, and residential purposes.</p></p>
