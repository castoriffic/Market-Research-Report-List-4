<p><p>Part Transfer Robots are used in manufacturing and assembly processes to transfer parts from one location to another with precision and efficiency. There are several types of Part Transfer Robots in the market, including Articulated Robots, SCARA Robots, Delta Robots, and Cartesian Robots. Articulated Robots have multiple joints and can move in a wide range of motion. SCARA Robots have high speed and precision for assembly tasks. Delta Robots are ideal for high-speed pick and place applications. Cartesian Robots have a simple design and are used for linear movements in a specific plane.</p></p>
