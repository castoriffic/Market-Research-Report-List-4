In ABS systems, these sensors monitor hydraulic pressure to prevent wheel lock-up. In airbags, they detect impact force to deploy airbags at the right time. In TPMS, they monitor tire pressure for safety. Additionally, they are used in engine control systems, HVAC systems, power steering, and transmission for monitoring and controlling pressure levels.</p></p>
