<p><ul><li>Wafer Tester</li><li>Packaged Device Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IC tester market can be divided into two main types: Wafer Tester and Packaged Device Tester. Wafer Tester is used to test integrated circuits while they are still in wafer form before being cut and packaged. This type of tester is essential for ensuring the quality and functionality of the ICs. On the other hand, Packaged Device Tester is used to test ICs after they have been packaged, simulating real-world conditions to ensure proper functioning before being used in electronic devices. Both types of testers play a crucial role in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
