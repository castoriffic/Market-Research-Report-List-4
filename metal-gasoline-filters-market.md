<p><p>The Metal Gasoline Filters market includes two main types - In-Line Type and Element/Cartridge Type filters. In-Line Type filters are installed directly in the gasoline fuel line to remove impurities and debris before reaching the engine. Element/Cartridge Type filters consist of replaceable cartridges or elements that trap contaminants from the gasoline. Both types of filters play a crucial role in maintaining the cleanliness and efficiency of the engine by preventing harmful particles from entering and causing damage.</p></p>
