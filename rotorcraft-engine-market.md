<p><p>Rotorcraft engines are categorized into two main types: Multiple Rotor and Single Rotor. Multiple rotor engines utilize more than one rotor system, commonly found in helicopters with twin or coaxial rotors. These engines provide increased stability and maneuverability. On the other hand, Single Rotor engines are more common and are used in traditional helicopters with a single main rotor system. They are simpler in design and easier to maintain, making them popular in various applications such as commercial and military rotorcraft.</p></p>
