<p><p>MEP Engineering Software market can be classified into two main types: BIM MEP Software and CAD MEP Software. BIM MEP Software utilizes Building Information Modeling technology to create detailed and accurate 3D models of mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems within a building project. On the other hand, CAD MEP Software focuses on Computer-Aided Design tools to draft and design MEP systems in 2D or 3D formats. Both types of software are essential in streamlining the design and coordination of MEP systems in construction projects.</p></p>
