<p><p>HPLC chips are used in chromatography to separate and analyze compounds. There are two main types of HPLC chip markets: Single Phase and Three Phase. Single Phase HPLC chips consist of one stationary phase, making them simpler and more cost-effective for routine analysis. On the other hand, Three Phase HPLC chips utilize three different phases to achieve higher separation efficiency and resolution, making them ideal for complex samples and research applications. Both types of HPLC chips offer different benefits depending on the specific analytical needs of the user.</p></p>
