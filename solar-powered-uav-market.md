<p><p>The solar-powered UAV market consists of three main types: fixed-wing UAVs, rotorcraft UAVs, and umbrella UAVs. Fixed-wing UAVs are the most common type and are known for their ability to cover long distances efficiently. Rotorcraft UAVs, like helicopters, are capable of vertical takeoff and landing, making them ideal for diverse mission requirements. Umbrella UAVs are a newer concept, characterized by a large surface area for solar panels, enabling them to stay airborne for extended periods. Each type caters to different operational needs within the solar-powered UAV market.</p></p>
