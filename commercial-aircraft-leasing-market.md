<p><p>Commercial aircraft leasing market consists of two main types- dry leasing and wet leasing. In dry leasing, the aircraft is provided without crew, maintenance, insurance, or other operational services. This option is suitable for airlines looking for more control over the aircraft operations. On the other hand, wet leasing involves the provision of the aircraft along with crew, maintenance, and insurance by the lessor. This option is preferred by airlines who need immediate capacity or want to mitigate operational risks.</p></p>
